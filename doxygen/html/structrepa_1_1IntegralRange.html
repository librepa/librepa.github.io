template<typename T, T min, T max, typename = typename std::enable_if<std::is_integral<T>::value>::type>
struct repa::IntegralRange< T, min, max, typename >

Type that behaves like an integral POD and can be restricted to a range.

Range is tested at construction time. Should not compile to any overhead on reasonable compilers and optimization levels.
