template<typename GBox>
struct repa::util::impl::Cart_StaticRankAssigner_impl< GBox >

Cartesian partitioning over a box-shaped domain.

The shape of the initial partitioning is defined by the dims of "comm". If it is a 1d Cartesian topology, the initial partitioning is 1d, etc.
