Detailed Description

template<typename T1, typename T2, typename = std::enable_if_t<!std::is_same_v<T1, T2>>>
struct repa::util::simple_variant< T1, T2, typename >

Simple variant of two distinct types.

Internally, consists of a union and an additional tag.
